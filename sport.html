Opening and Closing Odds Lines

Data packages include pre-match opening and closing odds lines. For your convenience, the date when the odds line was published by the bookmaker is indicated next to each coefficient.

Closing odds

These odds are the final odds offered by the bookmaker for the event, just before the start of the match.

Opening odds

These odds are the initial pre-match odds published by the bookmaker for the event.

Visual Distinction

For easy visual distinction, opening odds are highlighted in green , and closing odds are highlighted in red .

Below, you will find the available Odds Lines and Markets for export. 'Odds Line' refers to the main category, such as 1X2, Double Chance, Total Goals, etc. 'Odds Market' refers to the specific bet types within each Odds Line, such as 1X2 Full Time, 1X2 1st Half, 1X2 2nd Half, etc.

Please note that if an Odds Market is unavailable (i.e., the chosen bookmaker doesn't provide that market), its name will appear in light grey color.
