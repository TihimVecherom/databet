Welcome to the Database Creator

The Database Creator is a powerful tool designed to help you easily create, store, modify, and update sports betting databases.

With access to data from 33 bookmakers and 13 seasons, you can customize your databases to suit your unique needs and preferences.

Our goal is to provide you with a user-friendly service that empowers you to make informed betting decisions based on accurate, up-to-date data.

Benefits of Using the Database Creator:

  • Comprehensive data coverage

    Access historical odds and results from 33 bookmakers and 13 seasons.

  • Regular updates

    Enjoy 24 months of updates, ensuring your databases remain accurate and current.

  • Easy to use

    Create and manage your databases with our user-friendly interface and tools.

  • Customization

    Tailor your databases to your specific needs by choosing your preferred sports, bookmakers, and timeframes.

Features of the Database Creator

Data from 33 bookmakers

Access data from a wide range of bookmakers to diversify your betting options.

16 Seasons

Get comprehensive historical data, spanning 16 seasons to identify trends and patterns.

8 Sports

Choose from 8 different sports, covering a vast array of betting opportunities.

2,500,000 events

Gain insights from an extensive database with over 2.5 million events.
